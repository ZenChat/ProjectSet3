About Us

We are a group of highschoolers who participate in the program Girls Who Code.

Our names are: DaShelle Hall, Assitan Ndiaye, Vivian Nguyen, Lawren Francis, and Rhema Efik.

This website is our final project for the program.

Our website is about people with mental illnesses coming together and relating about personal issues that affect everyone in their everyday lives.

If you have any additional questions please visit our "Talk To Us" page.
